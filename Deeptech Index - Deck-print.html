07 — Methodology

How the index is built

08 / 08
01

Standardize

Each of the 23 indicators is converted to a z-score — distance from the 26-country mean in standard deviations. Zero is exactly average.

02

Aggregate

Indicator scores are averaged into six pillars. Sub-pillars (Ecosystem, Policy, Output) are averaged first so each theme carries equal weight.

03

Composite

The overall index is the equal-weighted mean of the six pillars. Countries are ranked overall and within their continent.
